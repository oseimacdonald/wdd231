NP Membership Benefits

Our NP Membership is designed specifically for non-profit organizations to help them connect with the business community.

  • Business listing in our online directory
  • Access to member-only networking events
  • Monthly newsletter subscription
  • Social media mentions
  • Volunteer opportunity postings
  • Community engagement opportunities
  • Non-profit resource center access
×

Bronze Membership Benefits

Our Bronze Membership offers essential benefits for small businesses looking to establish a presence in the community.

  • All NP Membership benefits
  • Enhanced business listing with logo
  • 10% discount on chamber events
  • Access to business training workshops
  • Member-to-member discounts
  • Quarterly business spotlight opportunity
  • Business referral program access
  • Annual business directory inclusion
×

Silver Membership Benefits

Our Silver Membership provides enhanced visibility and networking opportunities for growing businesses.

  • All Bronze Membership benefits
  • 20% discount on chamber events
  • Priority listing in directory
  • Featured in monthly member spotlight
  • Complimentary tickets to two major events annually
  • Access to exclusive Silver member networking events
  • Business referral program
  • Enhanced social media promotion
  • Business mentoring sessions
×

Gold Membership Benefits

Our Gold Membership offers premium benefits for established businesses seeking maximum exposure and influence.

  • All Silver Membership benefits
  • 30% discount on chamber events
  • Premium placement in directory and website
  • Featured in quarterly newsletter profile
  • Complimentary tickets to four major events annually
  • Speaking opportunities at chamber events
  • VIP access to annual gala
  • Board committee participation opportunities
  • Customized business introductions
  • Priority access to sponsorship opportunities
  • Executive networking events
  • Media exposure opportunities
